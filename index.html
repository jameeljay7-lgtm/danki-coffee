Coffee Science

Artisanal Brewing Guide

Maximize the flavors of Danki Coffee beans with our recommended recipes for each brewing device.

French Press

  • Grind size: Coarse
  • Ratio: 60g / Litre
  • Brew time: 4 mins

Pre-heat your press. Add coffee, saturate with hot water (94°C) and stir. Close the lid and press gently after exactly four minutes.

Pour Over (V60)

  • Grind size: Medium-Fine
  • Ratio: 15g / 250g
  • Brew time: 3 mins

Rinse paper filter. Pour hot water in slow concentric circles. Bloom for 30 seconds, then complete the pour with a steady stream.

Espresso

  • Grind size: Fine
  • Ratio: 18g / 36g
  • Brew time: 28-32 secs

Distribute and tamp evenly. Aim for a double espresso extraction of around 36g of liquid gold within 30 seconds for optimal sweetness.

Moka Pot

  • Grind size: Medium
  • Ratio: Fill basket
  • Brew time: 5 mins

Fill the bottom chamber with hot water to the safety valve. Add coffee to the basket without packing. Brew over low heat until it flows smoothly.
