The Danki Difference

Why Our Coffee?

Every bag tells a story of craft, quality, and an uncompromising love for great coffee.

Single Origin

Sourced directly from the highlands of Tanzania, our beans carry the unmistakable terroir of East African soil — rich, deep, and complex.

Small Batch Roasted

Every batch is roasted by hand in Dar es Salaam. We never mass-produce. Each roast is treated as a unique craft, preserving every nuanced flavor.

Made with Love

Danki Coffee is a family business. From Catherine Kikkides and the Danki team — we pour our heart into every single bag we roast and sell.
