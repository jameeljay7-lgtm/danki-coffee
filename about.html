Our Story

Born in Dar es Salaam. Roasted to Perfection.

Danki Coffee started with a simple vision: to bring the world's finest 100% Arabica beans to Tanzania, roasted with an uncompromising dedication to quality.

Every small batch is roasted by our master artisans to bring out the unique, nuanced flavors of the terroir. It's not just coffee. It's love.
