Our Story

A family passion roasted into every single bean

Who We Are

Born from Love, Brewed with Purpose

Danki Coffee began as a dream — a dream to bring truly exceptional, 100% Arabica coffee to the people of Tanzania and beyond.

Founded by Catherine Kikkides in the heart of Dar es Salaam, our family business is built on a simple belief: that every cup of coffee should tell a story of quality, care, and craft.

Operating under DANKI INVESTMENTS, we source the finest Arabica beans from the lush highlands of Tanzania — a land blessed with volcanic soil, cool mountain air, and generations of coffee-growing heritage. Every batch is roasted right here in Dar es Salaam, where the aromas of freshly roasted beans fill our workshop and fuel our passion.

Catherine's vision was never just about selling coffee. It was about creating a connection — between the farmer and the cup, between tradition and innovation, between a mother's love and the warmth of a perfect brew.

It's not just coffee, it's love.

From our family to yours, every bag of Danki Coffee carries the promise of premium quality, ethical sourcing, and the unmistakable flavour of Tanzania's finest.

From Farm to Cup

Our Process

Every step is guided by passion, precision, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

01

Sourcing

Our beans are carefully sourced from the rich volcanic highlands of Tanzania — Kilimanjaro, Arusha, and Mbeya — where altitude and climate create the perfect conditions for exceptional Arabica coffee.

02

Selecting

Only the finest cherries make the cut. Each harvest is hand-picked at peak ripeness, ensuring that every bean meets our exacting standards for flavour, size, and quality.

03

Roasting

In our Dar es Salaam roastery, we roast in small, artisan batches. Each roast is carefully monitored to unlock the bean's full spectrum of flavour — from bright citrus notes to deep chocolate undertones.

04

Delivering

Freshness is everything. Once roasted, our coffee is packed and delivered straight to your door — so every cup you brew is as fresh and flavourful as the day it was roasted.

What Drives Us

Our Values

The principles at the heart of every bean we roast.

Quality

We never compromise. From sourcing to roasting to packaging, every step is held to the highest standard. Only 100% premium Arabica beans make it into a Danki bag.

Family

Danki Coffee is a family business at its core. We treat our farmers, our team, and our customers as extensions of our own family — with warmth, respect, and genuine care.

Craft

Coffee is an art. We approach every roast as a craft — honouring traditional methods while embracing innovation to deliver a cup that's truly extraordinary.
